About Me

I'm Cláudio Bucene, a naturally curious technology enthusiast passionate about discovering how each line of code, system, and process connects to drive business securely and intelligently. I work at the intersection of IT auditing, cybersecurity, and innovation, helping organizations strengthen their digital controls, reduce risks, and increase trust in their technological infrastructures. I believe that technology only makes sense when it is secure, transparent, and well-governed—which is why I dedicate myself to building bridges between the technical and strategic sides of companies. With experience in IT Audit, IT Governance (GRC), and regulatory compliance, I draw inspiration from the COBIT, ISO 27001, and NIST frameworks to transform data and systems into intelligent decisions. Outside of spreadsheets and firewalls, I am driven by challenges, new ideas, and the desire to build a more ethical, innovative, and resilient Mozambican digital ecosystem.
